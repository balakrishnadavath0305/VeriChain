ABSTRACT

An AI-enhanced application using Polygon blockchain to automate and secure proof-of-existence. Anchors SHA-256 hashes on-chain for verifiability, uses Gemini AI for classification, and IPFS for permanent access.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Traditional document certification is highly vulnerable to forgery and data loss from centralized databases, relying on slow, costly, and manual verification processes.

OBJECTIVES
  • Eliminate centralized points of failure.
  • Provide cryptographic proofs.
  • Ensure instant verification.
  • Protect privacy via client-side hashing.
PROPOSED STATEMENT

A Web3 application where users hash files locally. Hashes are anchored immutably on Polygon. IPFS hosts public files, while Gemini AI provides document analysis before certification.

METHODOLOGY
  • 1. File Upload: Processed & hashed locally.
  • 2. AI Analysis: Classifies & checks exact fraud risk.
  • 3. IPFS Storage: Files uploaded for CID.
  • 4. Blockchain: Smart Contract stores Hash + CID.
  • 5. Verification: Direct on-chain query confirms authenticity.
ADVANTAGES
  • 100% Immutable and Tamper-Proof
  • Decentralized & Privacy-Preserving
  • AI-Assisted Easy Auditing
APPLICATIONS
  • Academic Degree Issuance & Verification.
  • Real Estate & Land Registry Records.
  • Supply Chain Authenticity Proofs.
